# Safety Vote - User Guide

## Welcome to Safety Vote

Safety Vote is a secure digital voting system designed for CIPA (Internal Accident Prevention Committee) elections. This guide will help you navigate the platform and cast your vote safely.

## Getting Started

### 1. Accessing the Platform
- Visit: https://safety-vote.example.com
- You'll see the home page with login options

### 2. Logging In (Voter)

#### Step 1: Enter Your Email
```
1. Click "Enter Election"
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click "Send Magic Link"
```

#### Step 2: Check Your Email
```
1. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
2. Look for email from "Safety Vote"
3. Click the magic link button or copy the link
```

#### Step 3: Verify Your Identity
```
1. The link will take you to verification page
2. You may be asked to enter your CPF (Brazilian ID)
3. Click "Verify" to confirm your identity
```

#### Step 4: Session Created
```
1. You're now logged in securely
2. No password to remember
3. Session expires after 4 hours of inactivity
```

### 3. Navigation

#### Home Page
- **Active Elections**: See elections you can participate in
- **My Votes**: See your voting history (anonymized)
- **Help**: Quick links to support

#### Election Page
- **Election Title**: Name of the election
- **Time Remaining**: When voting closes
- **Instructions**: How to vote
- **Start Voting**: Begin your voting process

## Voting Guide

### Before You Vote

**Check the Election Details:**
- Read the election title and description
- Note the candidates running
- Understand the voting rules
- Check if voting is still open

**Ensure Your Privacy:**
- Vote in a private location
- Keep your screen away from others
- Don't share your voting link

### Step-by-Step Voting

#### Step 1: Review Candidates
```
1. You'll see all candidates listed
2. Each candidate has:
   - Name
   - Position/Title
   - Brief biography
   - Optional photo
3. Review all candidates before deciding
```

#### Step 2: Select Your Choice
```
1. Click on your preferred candidate
2. Their card will be highlighted
3. You can change your mind and select another
```

#### Step 3: Choose Abstention (If Allowed)
```
1. Some elections allow "Abstain"
2. Click "Abstain" if you don't want to vote
3. This counts as participation
```

#### Step 4: Review Your Vote
```
1. Your selection is shown
2. Verify it's correct
3. Review one more time before confirming
```

#### Step 5: Confirm Vote
```
1. Click "Cast Vote" to finalize
2. Your vote is encrypted immediately
3. Confirmation message appears
```

#### Step 6: Vote Confirmation
```
1. You'll see: "✓ Vote Cast Successfully"
2. Your vote is now secure and anonymous
3. You cannot vote again in this election
4. Your vote cannot be traced to you
```

### Important Notes

- **One Vote Per Election**: You can only vote once
- **Encryption**: Your vote is encrypted immediately (AES-256)
- **Anonymity**: Your vote cannot be linked to you
- **No Verification**: You won't see proof of your specific vote
- **Time Limit**: Vote before the election closes
- **Browser Requirements**: Modern browser with JavaScript enabled

## After Voting

### Accessing Results

#### While Voting (If Elections Allows)
```
1. Go to "Results" tab
2. See live vote counts
3. See candidate rankings
4. See participation rates
```

#### After Voting Ends
```
1. Election status changes to "Finished"
2. Final results are published
3. See vote distribution
4. See elected candidates
5. See total participation
```

### Interpreting Results

**Vote Count**: Total votes received
**Percentage**: Portion of valid votes (excludes abstentions)
**Ranking**: Position (1st, 2nd, etc.)
**Elected**: Indicates winners

### Downloading Results
```
1. Click "Download Results"
2. Choose format: PDF, CSV, or JSON
3. Save file to your computer
4. Share or print as needed
```

## Privacy & Security

### Your Vote is Private

✅ **Protected By:**
- AES-256 encryption
- Immediate encryption after voting
- No vote-to-voter linking
- No cookies or tracking
- GDPR compliant storage

❌ **Not Stored:**
- Your voting choice linked to your ID
- Browser cookies with vote info
- IP address with vote
- Device fingerprints with vote

### Your Data

**What We Store:**
- Email (for authentication only)
- Timestamp of when you voted (not what you voted)
- Anonymized participation status
- Audit logs for security

**What We Don't Store:**
- Your password (magic link instead)
- Your specific vote
- Your device information
- Your browsing history

## Troubleshooting

### Problems Logging In

**Issue**: "Email Not Found"
```
Solutions:
1. Check email spelling
2. Verify email was registered
3. Ask RH department to register you
4. Contact support: support@example.com
```

**Issue**: "Magic Link Not Received"
```
Solutions:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Check email is correct
3. Wait 2 minutes (email takes time)
4. Request new magic link
5. Check with IT if email blocked
```

### Problems Voting

**Issue**: "Election Not Active"
```
Solutions:
1. Check voting period
2. Verify election has started
3. Verify election hasn't closed
4. Try refreshing page
```

**Issue**: "Already Voted"
```
Solutions:
1. You can only vote once
2. If accidental, contact admin
3. Admin can reset your vote
4. Then you can vote again
```

**Issue**: "Vote Not Submitted"
```
Solutions:
1. Check internet connection
2. Wait for page to respond
3. Try again after refresh
4. Check browser console for errors
5. Contact support if persists
```

### Technical Issues

**Issue**: "Page Not Loading"
```
Solutions:
1. Clear browser cache
2. Try different browser
3. Disable VPN/proxy
4. Check internet connection
5. Wait 5 minutes and try again
```

**Issue**: "Buttons Not Working"
```
Solutions:
1. Enable JavaScript in browser
2. Clear cookies
3. Try incognito/private mode
4. Update browser
5. Contact support
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Voting

**Q: Can I change my vote after voting?**
A: No, votes are final and cannot be changed. Vote carefully.

**Q: Is my vote completely anonymous?**
A: Yes, your vote is completely anonymous and encrypted.

**Q: Can anyone see how I voted?**
A: No, your vote is private and cannot be linked to you.

**Q: What if I make a mistake while voting?**
A: You have a review screen before confirming. Double-check before submitting.

### Results

**Q: When can I see results?**
A: After the election closes (or when allowed by election admin).

**Q: How do I know who won?**
A: Winners are marked in the results and receive certificates.

**Q: Can I download the results?**
A: Yes, results can be exported as PDF, CSV, or JSON.

### Security

**Q: How is my vote encrypted?**
A: AES-256 encryption (military-grade) is applied immediately.

**Q: Is my email safe?**
A: Yes, emails are encrypted and only used for login.

**Q: What about cookies and tracking?**
A: We don't use tracking cookies. Only session cookies for security.

### Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: Do I need a password?**
A: No, magic link authentication is used instead.

**Q: Can I vote on mobile?**
A: Yes, fully responsive and mobile-friendly.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No, works completely in web browser.
